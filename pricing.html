Common questions

Do I need my own Claude API key?
Yes — both Free and Pro use your Anthropic API key, which you supply once in Settings. PitchBox never proxies your queries; calls go straight from your browser to Anthropic. Pro removes our daily caps but you continue to pay Anthropic directly for what you use.
How does PitchBox handle my data?
Everything stays local in your browser. Scans, prospects, drafts, and your API key are stored in chrome.storage.local. Nothing is uploaded to our servers except your account email when you create a paid account. See Privacy Policy.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Email support@divergeix.com to cancel. No refunds for partial months but you keep Pro access until the end of the billing period.
