Your data, your control

PitchBox is built privacy-first. Here's exactly what happens with your data.

🔐 Local-first storage

Scans, prospects, and drafts live in chrome.storage.local on your device. Only your account email and payment record are uploaded to our servers.

🛡 GDPR & DPDP rights

Right to access, rectify, erase, port, and withdraw consent — exercise any of these by emailing privacy@divergeix.com. We respond within 30 days.

🏦 PCI-DSS payments

Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified, RBI regulated) handles all payment data. Your card details never touch our servers. Webhooks are HMAC-SHA256 signed.

☁️ ISO-aligned infrastructure

Backend hosted on Microsoft Azure (ISO 27001, ISO 27018, SOC 2 certified). Data residency in Central India. Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

🇮🇳 DPDP Act 2023 ready

Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act: explicit consent, purpose limitation, data minimization, breach notification, and Data Principal rights.

🚫 No training, no selling

Your scans and prospects are never used to train AI models. We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties.

Common questions

Do I need any external accounts or setup?
No. Everything's included in your subscription — sign in once and the AI features work out of the box. No third-party setup, no extra fees, no surprise costs.
How is my data protected?
Scans and prospects stay in your browser (chrome.storage.local). Only your account email + payment record are on our servers. We are GDPR + DPDP Act 2023 compliant. Backend on Microsoft Azure (ISO 27001 / SOC 2 certified). Payments via Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1, RBI regulated). See Privacy Policy.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Email support@divergeix.com to cancel. No refunds for partial months but you keep paid access until the end of the billing period.
Which payment methods?
UPI, GPay, all major Indian cards, net banking, and international Visa / Mastercard via Razorpay. All payments PCI-DSS compliant.
What happens if I downgrade?
You keep your current tier until the billing period ends, then drop to the new tier on the next renewal. Your saved prospects stay accessible regardless.
